## Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle with:

- Inventory shortages
- Overstocking
- Unpredictable demand
- Lack of centralized reporting
- Limited visibility into customer behavior

This project provides a complete analytics platform that helps decision-makers monitor performance, forecast future demand, optimize inventory, and improve business outcomes.


## Objectives

- Analyze retail sales performance
- Monitor inventory health
- Identify top-performing products
- Forecast future demand
- Evaluate customer behavior
- Generate AI-powered business insights


## Solution Architecture

Raw CSV Files
        ↓
 PostgreSQL
(Staging Layer)
        ↓
 Data Warehouse
        ↓
 Power BI Semantic Model
        ↓
 DAX Measures
        ↓
 Forecasting (Python)
        ↓
 Executive Dashboard
